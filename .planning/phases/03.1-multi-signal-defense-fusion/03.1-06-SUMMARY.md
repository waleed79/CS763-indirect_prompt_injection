## Decisions Made

- **SC-5 verification method:** Checked fused_fixed_0.5 ASR against min(individual signals) per tier; fused wins on all 3 measured tiers (T1, T2, T3) — SC-5 PASS confirmed at 3/3 not just the required 2/3.
- **DEF-02 finding is honest, not a bug:** def02 increases ASR because the system-prompt instruction-data separation reformats how context is injected, which may make the LLM more attentive to injected instructions. Deferred investigation to 03.1-07 rather than masking the counter-productive result.
- **fused_tuned_threshold row is a placeholder:** The LR meta-classifier trained in plan 03.1-04 has tuned_threshold=0.10, but re-running with that threshold requires a separate eval run. The row in ablation_table.json copies fused_fixed_0.5 numbers with a note — 03.1-07 will produce accurate numbers.
- **FPR=76% documented as a known utility cost:** Not a bug. The fused defense uses the bert_only threshold which aggressively filters; threshold tuning in 03.1-07 targets reducing FPR without sacrificing ASR.
